    Indicator available Proportion of girls and women who have undergone female genital mutilation/cutting by age at which mutilation/cutting occurred.
    Indicator description Number of newly recorded cases of female genital mutilation/cutting per 1,000,000 population
    Geographical coverage England
    Unit of measurement Newly recorded cases per 1,000,000 population
    Definitions Female genital mutilation/cutting (FGM/C) refers to “all procedures involving partial or total removal of the female external genitalia or other injury to the female genital organs for non-medical reasons" (World Health Organization, Eliminating Female Genital Mutilation: An interagency statement, WHO, UNFPA, UNICEF, UNIFEM, OHCHR, UNHCR, UNECA, UNESCO, UNDP, UNAIDS, WHO, Geneva, 2008, p.4).
